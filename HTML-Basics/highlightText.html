Learning Latin can provide practical benefits such as expanding vocabulary, understanding legal, medical, and scientific terminology, and gaining a better understanding of English grammar. However, these benefits alone aren't very good reasons to learn the whole language. Research shows that learning Latin leads to children scoring higher in other curriculum areas. Learning Latin can also broaden your horizons and help you understand the culture around you and its origins.4 Latin is valuable if you want to learn other Romance languages because those languages are derived at least partly from Latin.Additionally, learning Latin can improve language acquisition skills and make you better at speaking English.
